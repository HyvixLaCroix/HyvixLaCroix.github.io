What's this?

This webpage is designed to grab videos from e621.net and play them sequentially, hands-free for one less distraction switching to the next video while you watch. Simply enter in some tags and other preferences below, hit 'submit', and enjoy!

DISCLAIMER: This page is very new and early-stages, and there's tons of room for improvement! Please let me know about any issues or features you would like me to address.

MORE DISCLAIMER: e621 will not allow certain content to load without login information, which I have not implemented at this time. Currently these videos are (hopefully) just removed from the list, but you may have to skip them yourself now and again.
